Noritual Lab presents

TEA/ROOM FOR ONE

An immersive, 15-minute solo tea experience shaped around contemporary Japanese tea culture. You enter alone. You prepare your own bowl of matcha. Sound, scent, and sculptural elements surround you in a space designed for stillness.

Dates April 2–16, 2026
Venue GALERIE WASSER Berlin · Rosa-Luxemburg-Straße 16, 10178 Berlin, Germany
Duration 15 minutes — one person

About the work

A moment designed
for one.

There is no group here, no audience, no instruction beyond the gentle logic of the space itself. TEA/ROOM FOR ONE places you — alone — inside a carefully composed environment built around the meditative rhythms of Japanese tea practice.

You enter, orient yourself, and begin. A bowl. Matcha. Water at the right temperature. Around you, sound is layered with silence. Scent moves slowly through the room. Sculptural objects offer a visual language of pause.

This is an installation that asks nothing of you except presence. It is, in that sense, a gift of time — 15 minutes that belong entirely to you.
